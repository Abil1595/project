Cassava Chips, most selling product

Cassava chips are a crunchy, delicious snack made from fresh, handpicked cassava roots. Carefully sliced and fried to perfection, these chips offer a unique, satisfying flavor. Rich in natural fibers and gluten-free, they are a healthier alternative to traditional potato chips. Perfect for any occasion, enjoy them as a light snack or a flavorful side. Treat yourself to a wholesome crunch with every bite!

Garlic Murukku, a new product

Garlic Mini Murukku is a crispy, bite-sized traditional snack infused with the bold flavor of garlic. Made from rice flour and aromatic spices, each murukku is hand-twisted and fried to golden perfection. This gluten-free snack is perfect for munching anytime, offering a delightful crunch and rich taste. Ideal for festive occasions or everyday cravings, it's a must-try for garlic lovers. Savor the authentic taste of South India in every bite!

Cassavva Finger Chips, popular product

Cassava Finger Chips are a tasty and crispy snack made from fresh cassava roots, cut into long strips and perfectly fried. These crunchy treats are a great gluten-free alternative to regular fries, with a naturally rich flavor and satisfying texture. Ideal as a snack or side dish, they pair well with dips or can be enjoyed on their own. Packed with fiber and nutrients, they offer a wholesome and delicious crunch in every bite. Elevate your snack time with these irresistible cassava finger chips!

Orange Candy, a new product

Orange Candy offers a burst of tangy citrus flavor in every bite, delivering the refreshing taste of ripe, juicy oranges. These sweet and tangy treats are perfect for satisfying your candy cravings while giving a zesty twist to your day. Made with natural orange essence, they are a great on-the-go snack or a fun treat to share. Enjoy the vibrant taste and uplifting sweetness with each delightful piece. Brighten up your day with our irresistible orange candy!
